Research

My research integrates machine learning with hydrologic and hydraulic modeling to advance data-driven, scalable frameworks for assessing and improving the performance and resilience of urban stormwater infrastructure.

Urban Hydrology Urban Water Systems Hydrologic & Hydraulic Modeling Artificial Intelligence for Stormwater Stormwater Infrastructure & GSI Resilience Data-Driven Infrastructure Planning

Research Methods & Approaches

  • Hydrologic and hydraulic simulation modeling (e.g., SWMM, HEC-RAS, MIKE platforms)
  • Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence for water resource prediction and optimization
  • Statistical analysis, time-series modeling, and uncertainty quantification
  • Spatial and geospatial analytics integrating GIS and urban datasets
  • Integration of monitoring data, remote sensing, and model outputs for decision support

Tools & Technologies

  • Python (NumPy, Pandas, scikit-learn)
  • Machine learning and AI frameworks: TensorFlow, PyTorch
  • GIS and spatial tools: ArcGIS, QGIS
  • Hydrologic and hydraulic models: SWMM, HEC-HMS, HEC-RAS

Applications

  • Predictive modeling of urban flooding and stormwater system response
  • Performance assessment and risk classification of Green Stormwater Infrastructure
  • Data-driven planning and prioritization of stormwater investments
  • Climate-adaptive and resilient urban water infrastructure design

Keywords
