Publications

My scholarly work supports the development of scalable, evidence-based decision tools for stormwater and green infrastructure systems. Across my publications and presentations, I focus on applied artificial intelligence and data-driven modeling approaches that integrate monitoring data, system design and environmental context to improve infrastructure performance forecasting, maintenance prioritization, and long-term resilience.

Selected works below highlight contributions most aligned with my proposed U.S.-based endeavor in AI-enabled stormwater infrastructure performance assessment and risk-informed management.
